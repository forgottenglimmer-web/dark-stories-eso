Mystery #1

The Scenario

A plane crashes exactly on the border of Spain and Portugal. Every single person dies. However, two people survive.

How is this possible?

Clue 1: Think about the word "Single."

Clue 2: Does it refer to a number, or a status?

Solution #1

The Answer

The two survivors were married.

The Logic

In English, the word "single" can mean "only one," but it also means "unmarried." So, every unmarried person died, but the married couple survived!

Mystery #2

The Scenario

A man is found dead in the middle of the desert. He is clutching a small piece of straw (hay) in his hand. There are no tracks or footprints anywhere near him.

How did he get there?

Clue 1: Look up. How do people travel without touching the ground?

Clue 2: Why would someone be holding a piece of straw? Is it a game?

Solution #2

The Answer

He fell from a hot air balloon.

The Logic

He was in a hot air balloon with other people. The balloon was crashing because it was too heavy. They threw out their clothes and bags, but it was still falling. They decided to "draw straws" (a game of chance). The person who got the shortest straw had to jump to save the others. He lost.

Mystery #3

The Scenario

A man lives on the 10th floor. Every morning, he takes the elevator to the ground floor. When he returns, he takes the elevator to the 7th floor and walks the rest of the way up. On rainy days, he goes all the way to the 10th.

Why does he do this?

Clue 1: Is it because he wants exercise? (No).

Clue 2: Think about his physical height.

Clue 3: Why does the rain change things? What do people carry when it rains?

Solution #3

The Answer

The man is a dwarf (very short).

The Logic

He is too short to reach the button for the 10th floor on the elevator panel. He can only reach as high as the 7th floor button.

The Rain: On rainy days, he carries an umbrella, which he uses to poke the button for the 10th floor!

Mystery #4

The Scenario

A man receives a package in the mail. He opens it, finds a human arm inside, examines it, and then throws it in the trash. He is very happy.

Why is he happy?

Clue 1: He was not a killer. This was an agreement.

Clue 2: He was a survivor of a shipwreck with two other friends.

Clue 3: Did they have food on the island?

Solution #4

The Answer

It was proof of a "Suicide Pact" or survival agreement.

The Logic

Three men were stranded on an island. To survive, they agreed to eat their own arms. One was a doctor; he cut off the arms of the other two first. Before it was his turn, they were rescued. The other two men demanded he keep his promise. He had his arm surgically removed later and mailed it to them to show he was fair.

Mystery #5

The Scenario

A man is found dead inside a giant metal "star" floating in the dark. He was not murdered, and it was not a suicide. He was just doing his job.

What was his job?

Clue 1: Where do you find "stars" and "darkness"?

Clue 2: What kind of "metal star" do humans build?

Solution #5

The Answer

He was an Astronaut.

The Logic

The "metal star" is a Space Station. He was an astronaut working in orbit. He died because his oxygen ran out or there was a mechanical failure. In the dark of space, the station looks like a moving star to people on Earth.
